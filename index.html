About AI LAB

Our lab studies AI-based sensing and localization using signals from wearable and wireless devices. We analyze various human-related signals to estimate location, movement, and physiological states, developing intelligent systems that understand how people interact with their environment.

Localization

Researching AI-based methods to estimate human location and movement.

Machine & Deep Learning

Developing models that learn from multimodal signals using deep learning techniques.

Bio-signal Analysis

Analyzing PPG and wearable sensor data to understand physiological states.

Indoor Positioning

Building intelligent systems for accurate indoor tracking and spatial awareness.

Research Areas

PPG-based Threat Detection

Develop an automatic detection system for risk signals arising from emotional responses and transient physiological anomalies using low-cost sensors

PPG Signals Wearable device Threat detection

Wi-Fi CSI Based Localization and Pose Estimation

Estimate indoor position and generate accurate pose using amplitude and phase in WiFi CSI signals for advanced localization.

WiFi Signal Multi-task Model Localization

Geofencing-based Abnormal Path Detection

Using GPS data, we model the daily movement patterns of protected individuals and detect abnormal paths through GPR-based prediction and comparison.

GPS GPR(Gaussian Process regression) Localization

LLM-driven Situation Reasoning

Consolidates multimodal detections and history into operator-ready summaries and searchable reports for rapid response.

LLM SQL Situation Analysis Database

IMU-based Risk

Detects real-time physical risk by quantifying motion intensity and patterns from wearable accelerometers, enabling severity estimation on device.

Motion Sensing Risk Detection Wearable Devices
